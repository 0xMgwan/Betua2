**💡 What Are Prediction Markets?**

Prediction markets let you trade on the outcome of future events. If you think something will happen, you buy YES shares. If you think it won't, you buy NO shares. When the event resolves, winning shares pay out 1:1.

Example: "Will Manchester United win their next match?"
- If you buy YES at 60¢ and they win, you profit 40¢ per share
- If you buy NO at 40¢ and they lose, you profit 60¢ per share
